Rafay.

I am a Master's student majoring in Computational Mechanics, at the TUM School of Engineering and Design, part of the Technical University of Munich. I am also working as a student research assistant at the Chair of Computational Modeling and Simulation, where I work on methods for 3D scanning and mapping along with their implementation using different robotics systems.

From Jul-2020 to Oct-2021, I worked at the National University of Science and Technology, as part of the Integrated Navigation Terrestrial Electromagnetic Lab. I primarily worked on the design and development of drones (fixed wing and vertical take of and landing aerial vehicles) for urban environments. Additionally, I also worked on the development of models for flight simulator and tuning flight control through software-in-loop simulation.

I also have a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering from NED University of Engineering and Technology, I work as a student research assistant at the Department of Mechanical Engineering under the supervision of Dr.-Ing. Usman Allauddin, where I numerically investigated different techniques for heat transfer enhancement through jet impingement. [1], [2].

Technical University of Munich
Research Assistant - Chair of Computational Modeling and Simulation
Jul 2022 - Present

Working as student research assistant (HiWi) for the development of mapping system using LiDAR and RGBD cameras at Chair of Computational Modeling and Simulation.

  • Assisted deployment of mapping system on GO1 quadruped-robot. Streamline the process for calibrating, synchronizing, communicating, and data recording from the sensors.

  • Working on the implementation of pose graph optimization on the existing SLAM library and benchmarking localization performance of different slam packages.
National University of Science and Technology
Research Engineer - Integrated Navigation Terrestrial Electromagnetic Lab
Jul 2020 - Oct 2021

Contributed to a diverse set of projects as Research Engineer at Integrated Navigation Terrestrial Electromagnetic Lab at National University of Science and University.

Control Group:

  • Created model for VTOL, fixed-wing UAVs in x-plane flight simulator.
  • Conducted SITL (software in the loop) simulation by integrating x-plane flight simulator environment with mission planner (PX4) firmware, for tuning controller of fixed wing UAVs.

Design Group:

  • Created conceptual and preliminary design (aeronautics calculations) for fixed-wing UAVs and VTOL.
  • Led detail design activity to create CAD assembly in SolidWorks and exporting of manufacturing plans. Optimized aerodynamics and structural components by conducting CFD, FEA, FSI simulations using ANSYS.
  • Design test bench and propulsion system for DLE-120 engine.

Material Testing Group:

  • Conducted numeric simulation (ANSYS ACP), destructive and non-destructive (ultrasonic) testing of different composite samples with different composite layup schemes.
  • Collaborated in preparation of coursework and conducted lab sessions on calibrating and sizing defects through PAUT (phased array ultrasonic testing) using Omni scan MX8.
NED University of Engineering and Technology
Research Assistant - Department of Mechanical Engineering
Jul 2019 - Jan 2020

Assisted research activities in Mechanical Department of NED University under the supervision of Dr.-Ing. Usman Allauddin

  • Conducted a comparative study to assess different RANS turbulence models for prediction of the secondary peak in Nusselt number resulting from a hydraulic jump in jet impingement cooling.
  • Extensively reviewed literature and conducted numerical simulations related to impinging jets focusing on the influence of different types of fins, surface topology, pulsating/synthetic jets, and nanofluids on heat transfer characteristics. Collaborate on two peer-reviewed research papers based on findings.
  • Mentored undergraduate student groups, on their research project.
