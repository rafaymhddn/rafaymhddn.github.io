Rafay Mohiuddin (Under Development)

I am a Masters student majoring in Computational Mechanics, at the TUM School of Engineering and Design, part of the Technical University of Muncih. I am also working as a student research assistant at the Chair of Computational Modeling and Simulation, where I work on methods for 3D scanning and mapping along with thier implimentation using different robotics systems.

From Jul-2020 to Oct-2021, I worked at National University of Science and Technology, as part of Integrated Navigation Terrestrial Electromagnetic Lab. I primarily worked on the design and development of drones (fixed wing and vertical take of and landing aerial vehicles) for urban environments. Additionally I also worked on development of models for flight simulator and tuning flight controlled through software-in-loop simulation.

I have a Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from NED University of Engineering and Technology, I work as a research assistant at Department of Mechanical Engineering under supervision of Dr.-Ing. Usman Allauddin, where I numeriacally investigated different techniques for heat transfer enhancement through jet impingment [1], [2].

Technical University of Munich
Research Assistant - Chair of Computational Modeling and Simulation
Jul 2021 - Present

Working as student research assisting (HiWi) for the development of mapping system using LiDAR and RGBD cameras at Chair of Computational Modeling and Simulation.

  • Consolidated workflow for evaluating extrinsic camera parameters for spinning lidar and camera calibration, in absence of reference.
  • Implemented libraries for SLAM implementation on LiDAR. Updated code to support Ouster and Robosense LiDAR.
  • Working on dockerized implementation of libraries, sensor communication at distance using nimbro-network, and mobile deployment of mapping system utilizing GO1 robot.
National University of Science and Technology
Research Engineer - Integrated Navigation Terrestrial Electromagnetic Lab
Jul 2020 - Oct 2021

Contributed to a diverse set of projects as Research Engineer at Integrated Navigation Terrestrial Electromagnetic Lab at National University of Science and University.

UAV Design Group:

  • Created conceptual and preliminary design (aeronautics calculations) for fixed-wing UAVs and VTOL.
  • Developed setup for PID tuning of autopilot through SITL (software in the loop) simulation by integrating X-plane simulation models with mission planner firmware, for fixed wing UAV.

Control Group:

  • Created model for VTOL, fixed-wing UAVs in X-plane flight simulator.
  • Led detail design activity to create CAD assembly in SolidWorks and exporting of manufacturing plans.
  • Optimized aerodynamics and structural components by conducting CFD, FEA, FSI simulations using ANSYS

Material Testing Group:

  • Collected data obtained from ultrasonic testing of different composite samples with different composite layup schemes, conduct destructive testing and numeric simulation (ANSYS ACP) of respective samples.
  • Collaborated in preparation of course work and conducted lab sessions on calibrating and sizing defects through PAUT (phased array ultrasonic testing) using Omni scan MX8.
NED University of Engineering and Technology
Research Assistant - Department of Mechanical Engineering
Jul 2019 - Jan 2020

Assisted research activities in Mechanical Department of NED University under the supervision of Dr.-Ing. Usman Allauddin

  • Conducted a comparative study to assess different RANS turbulence models for prediction of secondary peek in Nusselt number resulting from a hydraulic jump in jet impingement cooling.
  • Extensively reviewed literature and conducted numerical simulations to asses influence of different types of fins, surface topology, pulsating/synthetic jets, and nanofluids on heat transfer characteristics of impinging jets. Collaborate on two research papers based in findings that got published in peer reviewed journals
  • Supervised research project of two undergraduate student group.
