Rafay Mohiuddin

Hello, I'm Rafay 👋 — a Master's graduate from the TUM School of Engineering and Design, with a focus on scientific computing, robotics, and 3D computer vision. My thesis research explored efficient and scalable methods for open-world 3D scene understanding (ICRA'25) using 2D foundation models and LLM.

I previously worked as a Robotics Research Intern at the Huawei Munich Research Center, focusing on lifelong localization and mapping, and as a Student Research Assistant at the Chair of Computational Modeling and Simulation, where I contributed to robotics and simulation-based projects.

My research interests spans robotics, 3D vision, machine learning, and physics simulation — with hands-on experience in developing and deploying research-driven solutions across these domains.

I'm currently working on a 3D generation related startup, and also consult and collobrate with AI-focused startups. Feel free to set up a call — I'm happy to discuss research, startups, robotics, 3D vision, LLMs, or potential collaborations.
